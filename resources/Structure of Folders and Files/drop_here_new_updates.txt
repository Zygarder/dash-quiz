Database Structure Recap & Relationships

quizzes

id, title

Relation: hasMany(questions)

questions

id, quiz_id, question_text, correct_option_id

Relation: belongsTo(quiz)

Relation: hasMany(answers)

answers

id, question_id, answer_text, is_correct

Relation: belongsTo(question)

quiz_records

id, user_id, quiz_id, score, completed_at

Relation: belongsTo(user), belongsTo(quiz)

dashers (users)

id, first_name, last_name, email, password

Relation: hasMany(quiz_records)
